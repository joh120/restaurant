History

A restaurant founded by the Santony Family in Rome Italy in 1924. 
Over the last 100 years Mamma Mia Pizzeria has become a known worldwide brand known for its Roman style dining experience and authentic Italian cuisine. 
Our tasty Italian food alongside our Roman style restaurant leaves diners feeling a taste of Italy! 
As of today Mamma Mia Pizzeria is located in 200 countries with over 4000 restaurants worldwide! So come on by and have a taste of Italy!





Mission


The mission of Mamma Mia Pizzeria is to give the world a taste of fine dining italian cuisine with a dining experience as that one will find in Rome!
         

Team


The team consist of the Santony Family. Owner Mario Santony and his two sons Marco and Pierre Santony alongside our other wonderful 40,000 worldwide employees that we have at hand!
